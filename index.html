Your phone charging solution

Elevate your venue experience by eliminating phones with low charge at no cost to you.

What does Brick do?

We offer an all in one solution for vending small powerbanks (portable chargers) for mobile phones to your customers.

What can Brick do for you?

01

Extend customer stays

Customers are more inclined to stay at your venue since they no longer have to worry about a low battery.

02

Bring in new customers

Showcase your venue to our app with over thousands of weekly users.

03

Eliminate phone charging requests

Your staff will no longer need to handle charging phones behind counters.

How does Brick work for your customers?

Customers will walk up to a Brick Station, scan the QR Code, select their payment, and then the machine dispenses the powerbank (mobile charger) to the user. Upon return, the customer puts the powerbank back in an empty slot on the Brick Station.
